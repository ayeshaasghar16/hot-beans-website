Who We Are

Hot Beans Web is a training organisation dedicated to teaching web development skills to beginners and aspiring developers. We focus on creating a supportive learning environment where students can grow their confidence and technical ability.

Our mission is to reduce the skills gap in the technology industry by making web development education accessible to everyone. We aim to give learners the opportunity to gain real-world skills that are useful in professional careers.

We believe that learning should be practical, not just theoretical. That is why we include hands-on projects and guided training sessions in our programme.

Our organisation continues to grow as more learners join and progress into successful careers in web development and IT.
