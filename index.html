Welcome to Hot Beans Web

Hot Beans Web is a professional web development training company that helps beginners learn how to design and build modern websites. We focus on teaching real skills that are used in the industry such as HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Our aim is to support learners step-by-step so they can gain confidence, build projects, and become job-ready developers. We also provide guidance for future careers in web development and IT.

Whether you are a complete beginner or improving your skills, we provide the tools and support you need to succeed.
