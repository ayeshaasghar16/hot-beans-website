Careers at Hot Beans Web

We are currently offering exciting opportunities for individuals who want to start a career in web development. This is a great opportunity for beginners who are passionate about technology and want to gain real experience.

As a junior web developer, you will learn how to create websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. You will also work on real projects which will help you build a strong portfolio for future job applications.

Full training and support will be provided, so no previous experience is required. We focus on helping learners develop skills at their own pace while gaining confidence in coding.

This role is ideal for anyone who wants to start a long-term career in the digital or technology industry.
